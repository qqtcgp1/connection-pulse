## Full Description (4000 chars max)

Connection Pulse is a lightweight network monitoring tool that tests TCP connectivity to your servers and services. Perfect for IT professionals, developers, and anyone who needs to keep an eye on server availability.

**Key Features:**

• Real-time TCP probing - Tests connectivity every 5 seconds
• Health status badges - Instantly see if servers are Optimal, Great, Good, Warning, Bad, or Down
• Latency tracking - View average and p90 latency over a rolling 5-minute window
• Success rate monitoring - Track connection reliability as a percentage
• Multiple targets - Monitor as many servers as you need
• Drag & drop reordering - Organize your server list your way
• Import/Export - Save and share your target configurations as JSON
• Dark & Light themes - Follows your system theme preference
• No account required - Works entirely offline on your device

**Health Categories:**

• OPTIMAL: 99.5%+ success, ≤15ms avg, ≤30ms p90
• GREAT: 99%+ success, ≤30ms avg, ≤80ms p90
• GOOD: 98%+ success, ≤80ms avg, ≤200ms p90
• WARN: 95%+ success rate
• BAD: 70%+ success rate
• DOWN: Below 70% success rate

**Use Cases:**

• Monitor trading servers and financial services
• Track game server connectivity
• Check VPN endpoint availability
• Verify cloud service health
• Test home server accessibility
• Network troubleshooting

**Privacy:**

Connection Pulse works entirely on your device. No data is sent to external servers. No account or login required. Your target list is stored locally on your device.

**Open Source:**

This app is open source. View the code at: https://github.com/qqtcgp1/connection-pulse
