# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** January 24, 2026

## Overview

Connection Pulse ("the App") is a network connectivity monitoring tool. This privacy policy explains how the App handles your data.

## Data Collection

**The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.**

Specifically:
- No user accounts or registration required
- No analytics or tracking
- No advertising
- No data sent to external servers
- No personal information collected

## Data Storage

The App stores only the following data locally on your device:
- **Target list**: The servers/hosts you configure for monitoring (name, hostname, port)
- **Session statistics**: Latency measurements and success rates (cleared when app closes)

This data:
- Never leaves your device
- Is stored in your device's app data folder
- Can be exported/imported as JSON files you control

## Network Connections

The App makes network connections only to:
- The target hosts/ports you explicitly configure for monitoring

These connections are TCP probes to test connectivity. No data is transmitted beyond the TCP handshake.

## Permissions

The App requires:
- **Network access**: To perform TCP connectivity tests to your configured targets
- **Storage access**: To save/load target configurations (optional import/export feature)

## Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks.

## Children's Privacy

The App does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
